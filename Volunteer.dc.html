Volunteer at CCA

加入我们，共同守护

CCA is built by volunteers. Every class and event depends on people who give their time.

We welcome Chinese language teachers, enrichment instructors, youth volunteers (age 10+), and event volunteers. CCA is a PVSA certifying organization (program paused May 2025). Apply below or email general@charlottechineseacademy.org.

Why volunteer? · 为什么加入我们？

The community runs on you

CCA runs on community. Teachers, parents, and students all contribute their time every Sunday to deliver an exceptional cultural learning experience.

Volunteering is a way to connect with the Chinese-American community, share your skills, and build your service record for school, college, or personal growth.
