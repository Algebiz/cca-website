Who we are

What is Charlotte Chinese Academy?

我们是谁？

CCA is a community nonprofit Chinese school founded in 1996 — today the largest Chinese school in North Carolina. We believe language is the bridge to culture, and every Sunday hundreds of families cross that bridge together.

Built by parents, run by volunteers, and sustained by a community that believes in what we do.
